Tera smart study buddy — Indian students ke liye banaya gaya!

🐍 VasuKi AI ek free AI-powered study assistant hai jo Indian students ki padhai mein madad karta hai. Hindi, English, aur Hinglish mein baat kar sakte ho!

Hamara Mission

India mein crores students hain jinhe padhai mein help chahiye. Lekin acche tutors bahut mehenge hain. VasuKi AI har student ko free mein ek smart study buddy dena chahta hai — jo 24/7 available ho, koi bhi subject samjhaye, aur bilkul apni bhasha mein baat kare!

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Information We Collect

VasuKi AI collects minimal information to provide our services. We do not require user registration or personal information to use our basic chat service.

  • Chat messages you send to VasuKi AI (processed to generate responses)
  • Basic usage analytics (page views, session duration)
  • Information you voluntarily provide through our contact form

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and improve our AI chat service
  • Respond to your queries and support requests
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve user experience
  • Display relevant advertisements (Google AdSense)

Data Storage

Chat conversations are not permanently stored on our servers. Each session is independent and your chat history is cleared when you close the browser tab.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Groq AI: For processing chat messages and generating responses
  • Google AdSense: For displaying advertisements
  • Vercel: For website hosting

Cookies

We use cookies to improve your experience. Google AdSense may use cookies to display relevant ads. You can control cookie settings through your browser.

Children's Privacy

VasuKi AI is designed for students aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
